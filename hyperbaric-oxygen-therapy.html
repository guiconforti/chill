What is hyperbaric oxygen therapy?

Hyperbaric oxygen therapy (HBOT) at ChillRx Delray is a 60-minute session inside a chamber pressurized to about 1.5 atmospheres, with optional supplemental oxygen. Members describe it as the most relaxing hour of their week and use it to support recovery, energy, sleep, and pre- and post-surgical healing.

Who it's for

HBOT is for anyone focused on recovery and healing — including before and after surgery (with your surgeon's clearance), athletes and active people, and those who want better energy and sleep. It is consistently one of the most loved services at ChillRx.

Benefits

  • A deeply calming, restful 60 minutes
  • Supports recovery and healing, including pre- and post-operative care
  • Members report better energy and clearer thinking afterward
  • Often improves sleep quality
  • A relaxing complement to cryo, red light and compression

What to expect

  1. Settle in. You get comfortable inside the chamber — bring a podcast or simply rest. Many people nap.
  2. Gentle pressurization. The chamber pressurizes gradually to about 1.5 ATA; you may feel your ears adjust, like on a plane.
  3. Relax for ~60 minutes. You breathe normally — with optional supplemental oxygen — while your body soaks in the session.
  4. Ease back out. The chamber depressurizes gently and you leave feeling rested and clear-headed.

Results & timeline

Many members feel calm and clear-headed immediately, with better sleep that night. For recovery and healing goals, benefits build over a series of sessions. Your team will help you plan a cadence that fits your goal. Results vary by person.

Risks & considerations

HBOT is generally well tolerated. It may not be appropriate with certain ear, sinus, lung or other medical conditions, and you should always clear pre- or post-surgical use with your surgeon. Share your health history with the ChillRx team. This service is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease.
